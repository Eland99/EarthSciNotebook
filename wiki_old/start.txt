======Greg Maurer's research wiki======

{{:hiddencanyon:hcimg_3855_scaled_.jpg?225  |Hidden Canyon Met datalogger}}

This is the wiki for [[http://pronghorns.net/about.html|Greg Maurer's]] research projects, which are focused on biogeochemical cycling in the seasonally snow-covered ecosystems of the western U.S. This research is based at the [[http://bioweb.biology.utah.edu/bowling/|Bowling lab]], in the [[http://www.biology.utah.edu|University of Utah Biology Department]].

**Cautionary note:**
All data, code, figures, results, and discussions are presented in their raw, preliminary form. They have not been peer reviewed and are provided without guarantee of quality, accuracy, or safety.

=====Using this site===== 
This [[wp>wiki]] records day to day research activities for various research projects, including field and lab notes, method development, preliminary results, data analysis code, figures, photos, and discussions. It is essentially an open version of the electronic [[wp>lab notebook]] for [[http://pronghorns.net/about.html|Greg Maurer's]] research projects. Because it is a wiki, this site can also be used for scientific collaboration. Those who would like to add to, comment on, or edit content on this site should email <admin@pronghorns.net>. **Off-topic posts will be removed.**

Before you begin take a look at:
  * [[wiki:standards|Rules and conventions]]
  * The [[faq|FAQ]]
  * If you would like to contribute or edit see the [[wiki:syntax|DokuWiki Syntax]] page to learn how.
  * You can test your editing in the [[playground:playground|wiki playground]].
